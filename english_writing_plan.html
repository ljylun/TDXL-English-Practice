📈各档得分特征（语言准确性）
  • 4-5分（满分档）：句式变化丰富，偶见小错，不影响表达
  • 3分（良好档）：有少量语法错，主干句式正确，逻辑清晰
  • 2分（中等档）：错误较多但可理解，缺乏多样性
  • 1分（及格档）：严重错误影响阅读，词汇使用不当

🎯话题审题三步法
  • 识别题型： 观点型（Do you agree that...？）→ 明确表态；描述型（Describe the phenomenon...）→ 分析现象；建议型（What measures should be taken...？）→ 提出方案
  • 提取关键词： 圈出名词核心词+动词方向词，如"discuss the impact of technology on education"→ technology, impact, education
  • 确定立场： 不要中立骑墙！选择一个明确立场（正面或批判性支持），论证更有力，得分更高

模板化与反模板化策略
用框架提升速度，用变通避免僵化
⚠️ 注意：纯模板套用会导致内容空洞、脱离题目，最终被扣内容分。正确做法是：框架稳定 + 内容灵活，保留结构骨架，根据题目填入实质性内容。
📄标准三段式框架（适用90%题型）
P1 【开头段 — 引入+表态】
[话题引入句] + [明确立场句]

P2 【主体段 — 论证+举例】
[中心论点句] + [原因/数据/举例] + [进一步分析]

P3 【结尾段 — 总结+展望】
[重申立场] + [建议/呼吁] + [展望未来]
🚀三种开头模板（选一使用）

① 现象引入法

In recent years, [topic] has become a topic of considerable debate. While some argue that..., I firmly believe that [your position].

② 数据引入法

According to recent studies, [relevant fact/trend]. This phenomenon has prompted people to reconsider [topic].

③ 问题引入法

What role does [topic] play in our modern life? This question has aroused wide concern as [brief context].
🏁两种结尾模板（选一使用）

① 总结呼吁型

In conclusion, [restate main point]. It is high time that we [call to action] in order to [desired outcome].

② 展望未来型

To sum up, [brief summary]. Only through [key measure] can we hope to build a better future where [vision].
❌ 避免："As we all know..." "Last but not least..." 这类过度使用的套话
🔄反模板化技巧：让作文"活"起来
普通写法（模板化）升级写法（个性化）提分效果
Technology has advantages and disadvantages. Technology reshapes our daily routines, yet this transformation comes with hidden costs to human connection. +内容分
We should protect the environment. Meaningful environmental change begins not with grand policies but with individual choices made daily. +词汇分
In conclusion, I think... Ultimately, the evidence overwhelmingly suggests that... +表达分
For example, many people... A compelling illustration of this point is the fact that... +结构分

错误诊断与改进闭环
识别错误类型 → 找到根因 → 针对练习 → 验证改进
Error Type 01
主谓不一致 Subject-Verb Agreement
The number of students are increasing.
The number of students is increasing.

📌 规则："the number of" 后接单数谓语；"a number of" 后接复数

Error Type 02
时态混乱 Tense Inconsistency
Technology changes our life and made communication easier.
Technology changes our life and makes communication easier.

📌 规则：议论文全程用一般现在时，除非引用历史事实

Error Type 03
冠词错误 Article Misuse
Education plays important role in development of society.
Education plays an important role in the development of society.

📌 规则：play a/an role 是固定搭配；特指名词前加 the

Error Type 04
连接词误用 Connector Misuse
I like studying. Despite, I sometimes feel tired.
I like studying. However, I sometimes feel tired.

📌 规则："Despite" 后接名词/动名词，转折用 However/Nevertheless

Error Type 05
非谓语动词错误 Non-finite Verb
Faced with challenges, students should to try their best.
Faced with challenges, students should try their best.

📌 规则：情态动词后接动词原形，不加 to

Error Type 06
段落缺乏论证 Underdeveloped Argument
Technology is helpful. It is very useful for people.
Technology enhances productivity significantly; for instance, remote work tools enable collaboration across continents, saving both time and resources.

📌 原则：每个论点必须配具体例子或数据支撑

🔄改进闭环四步法
  • 写完后标记： 用不同颜色笔标注语法错、逻辑弱、词汇重复三类问题
  • 错误归类： 将所有错误归入上述6大类型，统计各类频率
  • 针对练习： 针对最高频错误类型，专项练习5-10句改错题
  • 重写验证： 次日重写同一话题，验证该类错误是否明显减少
