英语学术写作范文 · 六维度深度解析报告

AI & Employment — 适用于雅思写作 / 托福写作 / SCI-SSCI 学术论文教学

As artificial intelligence rapidly advances, concerns about job displacement have grown significantly. Some fear widespread unemployment, while others believe AI will create new opportunities and drive industrial upgrading. In my opinion, AI should be viewed as a transformative force that reshapes work rather than simply eliminates it.

On the one hand, AI does automate certain routine tasks, potentially displacing some workers in traditional industries. For example, manufacturing robots and automated customer service systems have already replaced many repetitive positions. On the other hand, it generates demand for new skills and creates emerging job categories in fields like AI development, data analysis, and human-machine collaboration. Moreover, industries such as healthcare and education are increasingly relying on AI to enhance, rather than replace, human expertise.

Therefore, the key lies in continuous learning and adaptation. Governments and businesses also share the responsibility of providing retraining programs to support affected workers. By embracing skill development and maintaining flexibility, workers can turn this challenge into an opportunity for career growth in the AI era.
